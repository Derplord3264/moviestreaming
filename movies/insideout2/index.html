Inside Out 2

Movie Description

"Inside Out 2" is the highly anticipated sequel to the 2015 animated hit from Pixar, directed by Kelsey Mann. Set to continue the emotional journey within the mind of now-teenage Riley, the film reintroduces us to Joy, Sadness, Anger, Fear, and Disgust as they navigate the complexities of adolescence. With new challenges and emotions emerging, the characters must work together to help Riley cope with the changes and experiences of growing up. The sequel promises to deliver a heartfelt and imaginative exploration of emotional growth, maintaining the blend of humor and depth that made the original film so beloved.
